# Example: Sales Outreach — B2B Cold Email Sequence

Domain skill: `sales-outreach`

---

## Inputs
- ICP: Head of Operations at Series A–C B2B SaaS companies, 50–500 employees
- Pain: manually reviewing AI agent output, no verification system
- Apollo search filters: title contains "Head of Operations" OR "VP Operations", industry = SaaS, headcount 50–500
- Brand voice guide (linked)
- Product one-liner: "Orchestrator — verified AI agent output, not just AI agent output"

## Deliverables
- [ ] 200 Apollo contacts sourced matching ICP filters
- [ ] 5-touch email sequence created in Apollo:
  - Day 1: cold intro (pain + proof)
  - Day 3: case study (result + metric)
  - Day 7: objection handle ("we already have agents")
  - Day 12: breakup ("last one")
  - Day 21: re-engage if no reply
- [ ] Subject line A/B variants set (≥2 per send)
- [ ] Sequence imported to Apollo and set to draft (not live)
- [ ] Test email sent and reviewed

## Verification
- Apollo contact list: 200 contacts, bounce rate ≤5% (Apollo estimate)
- Sequence imported to Apollo (screenshot of sequence editor)
- Test email received and rendered correctly (screenshot)
- Unsubscribe link present and functional in every email
- All UTM links resolve
- Subject line A/B variants visible in Apollo (≥2 per touch)
